Welcome to Crossroads Village

Crossroads Village is located at the SEQ of North Wickham Road and Pineda Causeway in the center of the demographically superior communities of Viera, Suntree, Merritt Island, West Viera, and the Beaches; the shortest distance, and quickest trip, between I-95 and the Beaches in all of Brevard County. Easily accessible from every direction.

revard County is presently home to 550,000 people, most of which reside less than 25 minutes from this centrally located, commercial village. Of the five Causeways serving the affluent Brevard County beach populations, Crossroads Village sits on the middle and shortest causeway, Pineda Causeway. The new and highly affluent communities in West Viera are now under construction west of I-95, solidifying Crossroads Village’s location as the finest, most central business location serving Brevard County. Come home to “The Center of Business in Brevard County, Florida”.
