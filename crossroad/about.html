About Us

Welcome to Crossroad Village

LOCATION: Crossroads Village is “The Center of Business in Brevard County, Florida” with its geographically-perfect location at the southeast corner of North Wickham Road and Pineda Causeway. “The Northern Gateway to the City of Melbourne, Florida”.

LOCATION: One mile west of Brevard County’s most affluent beach residents and Patrick Air Force Base. Less than two miles east of Brevard County’s newest Beach-to-Interstate 95 interchange, at mile marker #189, and to Viera Corporation’s newest western expansion communities in “West Viera”, now under construction. In the heart of Brevard County’s most upscale communities of Deer Lakes, Viera, Suntree, Merritt Island, and the Beaches.

LOCATION: The most direct, convenient, and accessible business location for all of Brevard County’s 550,000 residents to reach. For the convenience of their customers, clients, donors, and employees, national tenants American Cancer Society, Kindred Health Care, and Kelly Services have chosen Crossroads Village for their Brevard County Headquarters. A PNC Bank branch at CV’s to-be-signalized southern entry is among Crossroad Village’s family of tenants, which also includes regional medical and dental offices.
